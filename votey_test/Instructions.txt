Need to Know Information: 
Move green square (Maggie) with up, down, left right arrow keys to the different students (blue squares)
Hover over green square, changes to pink.
Question and professor dots will appear when Maggie overlaps a student.
Professor names are next to their appropriate dot.
Answer question by clicking on correct professor dot.
